#1: How can I connect my accounts?

How to connect a Yandex Music account?

1. Get your Yandex Music account's email and password.

2. Go on "Connected accounts" page and press "Connect Yandex Music account".

3. Fill in the form with the data.

4. Press "Add account". If your email and password are correct, the account will be connected and your library will be loaded.

How to connect a Spotify account?

1. Go on Spotify developer's web-site (https://developer.spotify.com/). Log in in your Spotify account and create a new app (https://developer.spotify.com/dashboard/) on a Dashboard page.

2. Get your Client ID,Client Secret. Set a redirect uri for your project. It can be a not existing uri, you will be redirected to this page during the registration on our site.

3. Return to our web-site, go to "Connected accounts" page and press "Connect Spotify account". Fill in the form with the data.

4. Press "Add account". You will be redirected to a new page. You should copy its url and paste it in the "Spotify code" field. If your data is correct, you won't get an "Illegal redirect_uri" message.

5. Press "Add account". If everything is correct, your albums will be loaded automatically.

It seems that my data is correct, but Spotify albums weren't loaded. What should I do?

1. Try adding users to your project on Spotify developer's web-site. Go to your project's page and press "Users and access" button. Try adding all your existing Spotify accounts and all the account that have been authenticated on Spotify desktop app or web-player on your computer. Wait for 5-10 minutes and try to add your account to our site again.

2. If that didn't work, add a nerhneiro@gmail.com user to your Spotify project.

3. If nothing of these helped, write to nerhneiro@gmail.com for help.

You can always change your Yandex Music and Spotify accounts information filling the forms again.
