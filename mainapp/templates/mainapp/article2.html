#2: How can I share tags with friends?

1. Go to "Friends" page. If you haven't added any friends, add some.

2. To add a new friend you need to find his account in form and send him a friend request by pressing the button "Send friend request". A friend will be added when he/her accepts the request.

3. Choose a friend you want to share tags with and press a "Share tags" button near his nickname.

4. Choose a tag/tags in form and press a "Share" button to send a tag share request. The request will be sent.

5. If your friend accepts the request from you, your tags will be added to his library.
